The Corporation of the City of Burlington is seeking the supply and delivery of newest model year for the following mowers:

  1. Three Wheel Drive Riding Greens Mower (estimated quantity 2)
  2. Three Wheel Drive Riding Rotary Trim Mower (estimated quantity 1)
  3. Seven Plex Contour Rotary Mower Area Mowers (estimated quantity 1)
  4. Turbine Debris Blower (estimated quantity 1)
  5. Powered Greens Aerator (estimated quantity 1)
  6. Four Wheel Drive Out Front Mower with 72 Inch Rear Discharge Deck (estimated quantity 4)
  7. Articulating Tractor with Mower decks and Accessories (estimated quantity 1)
  8. Zero Turn Mower with 60 Inch Rear Deck, Rear Discharge (estimated quantity 1)

Bidders may bid on any combination of items 1 through 8, and the contract may be awarded to multiple bidders, subject to being the lowest compliant bid.

Therefore, partial Bids will be accepted.
